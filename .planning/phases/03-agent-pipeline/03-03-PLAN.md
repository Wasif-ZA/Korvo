2. `scoreSeniority(title: string): number` — Map title keywords to seniority scores:
   - Senior/Lead/Staff/Principal IC: 20
   - Mid-level (no prefix or "Software Engineer"): 15
   - Junior/Associate/Intern: 10
   - Manager/Director: 12 (hiring power but may not reply personally)
   - VP/C-suite/Founder: 5 (too senior to reply to cold email)

3. `extractSignals(contact: ContactResult, targetRole: string, enrichment: CompanyEnrichmentData | null): ScoringSignals` — Composes signals from contact data + enrichment:

   - publicActivityScore: contact.publicActivity ? 15 : 5
   - emailConfidenceScore: high=15, medium=10, low=5
   - hiringSignalScore: enrichment?.hiringRoles matching targetRole ? 15 : 5

4. `scoreContact(signals: ScoringSignals): ScoreResult` — Sum all signals, clamp 0-100, map tone per D-10.
