# Design Decisions (v7: High-Density Blueprint)

## Solving the "Plain" Problem
- **Filler Geometry**: Use ASCII-style dot clusters (`· · ·`), box characters (`┌ ── ┐`), and plus signs (`+ + +`) to occupy the "voids" in the background.
- **Side Labels**: Add vertical or small horizontal metadata on the far edges of the content container.
- **Nested Borders**: Cards will often have an "inner border" or a secondary outline to add visual complexity.

## Tone & Vocabulary
- **Remove**: "AI-powered", "Smart", "Intelligent".
- **Add**: "Pipeline Execution", "Data Enrichment", "Contact Scrapers", "Tone Logic", "Queue Processing".
- **Why**: The user feels the "AI" mentions are wrong. We will focus on the *engineering* of the tool.

## Animation
- **Glitch & Pulse**: Subtle opacity flickers on dot clusters.
- **Section Connecting**: Lines that "extend" as the user scrolls.
- **Blinking Monospace Cursors**: For all technical labels.
